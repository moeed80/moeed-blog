### About the Company

I am the registered legal entity behind utilities like Blue Bubble Vault and Local Stitch. The company name itself is entirely sentimental, borrowed from a quiet town in Pakistan where I spent the first few years of my life running around near the old [Mangla Fort](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mangla_Fort). It is under this official corporate banner that I register my Apple Developer Account and legally publish all the software I build.

So far I've built a couple of lightweight, native macOS desktop applications, mostly leveraging local LLMs running right on my Mac, that do exactly what they say on the tin, without spying on you. I operate under a strict baseline of data sovereignty, meaning every line of code I write is optimized to execute 100% locally on your machine. I have no interest in your data, I maintain no mysterious cloud servers, and I do not track your usage. I simply build utilities to solve highly specific digital nuisances, packaging them neatly into software you can run with absolute peace of mind.

If you find any of these tools useful, do a kind thing to a neighbour. You may drop me a follow on [X/Twitter](https://x.com/moeed), though I hardly tweet now, but do read often, so if you @ me there, I'll likely see it at some point.

**Developer Support & Legal Compliance**  
*This section provides the mandatory documentation and contact pathways required for application notarization and Apple App Review compliance.*

**1. Privacy Policy**  

Because data sovereignty is the core pillar of my software design, the privacy policy for all applications published under this banner is straightforward:

- **Data Collection:** My applications do not collect, store, transmit, or monetize any personal data, analytical tracking information, or user files.
- **Local Processing:** All database querying, file processing, structural indexing, cryptographic hashing, and local LLM execution happen entirely on your physical machine.
- **Third-Party Access:** Because zero data is sent to external servers, no third parties ever have access to your information.

**2. User Support & Contact**
